Academic Exchange Programs
The Institute has continued to maintain and enhance the academic excellence it has had since its inception that has enabled it to have collaborative academic exchanges with the following International / National Universities / Institutions of academic eminence. MoUs have already been signed with most of these institutions and with the rest they are in process.
