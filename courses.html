MBA
IIIT Allahabad's MBA program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the field of management. The program is structured into four semesters, with each semester covering a range of topics related to finance, marketing, operations, and human resources management.

During the first two semesters, students will be exposed to courses such as Financial Accounting & Analysis, Business Statistics, Management Thoughts and Environment, Economics, Organizational Behavior, Research Methodology, and IT Infra. And Service Management. The third semester covers courses such as Strategic Management, Business Continuity Planning and Disaster Recovery Planning (BCP & DRP), and two elective courses from Elective I and Elective II options. Additionally, students will complete a summer training program. The fourth semester is focused on advanced topics such as Data Mining and Warehousing, International Business Management, and two more elective courses from Elective I and Elective II options. In this semester, students will also complete a master project, which is an opportunity for them to apply the skills they have learned to real-world business problems.

The elective courses are grouped into Finance, Information Technology, Marketing, Operations, and Human Resource Management. The Finance group offers nine elective courses, including International Financial Management, Security Analysis & Portfolio Management, and Behavioral Finance. The Information Technology group offers seven elective courses, including Cloud Computing and Business Intelligence. The Marketing group offers eight elective courses, including Digital Marketing and Consumer Behavior & CRM. The Operations group offers eight elective courses, including Total Quality Management and Purchasing and Supply Management. The Human Resource Group offers five elective courses, including Emotional Intelligence and Leadership and Strategic HRM. Overall, IIIT Allahabad's MBA program offers a diverse range of courses and electives that prepare students to become successful business leaders. The program provides a balance of theoretical knowledge and practical skills that are necessary for success in today's competitive business environment.
